Scripting

Operator Example
eq (equals) A -eq B will give false
ne (not equals) A -ne B will give true
gt (greater than) B -gt A will give true
ge (greater than or equals to) B -ge A will give true
lt (less than) B -lt A will give false
le (less than or equals to) B -le A will give false

AND, OR, NOT work too
